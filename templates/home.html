About JNMCH Portal

This secure medical reporting system enables efficient management and access to test reports. Features include secure patient registration, OTP-based authentication, automated report notifications, and role-based dashboards for different user types.

Data Privacy: All patient data is encrypted and secure. We comply with healthcare data protection regulations.
